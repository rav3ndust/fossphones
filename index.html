FOSSphones News Update: Linux Phone News - January 17, 2023

Thank you for visiting FOSSphones. We're glad you stopped by!

As promised, our FOSSphones Community is on its way to being up and running.

We are using some of my favorite decentralized technologies in the building of this part of FOSSphones, including xmpp, matrix, and good old-fashioned IRC.

I will post back here as soon as the setup is done. You are free to come join us at the FOSSphones Element Space if you would like to chat with fellow Linux Phone users, developers, and enthusiasts.
